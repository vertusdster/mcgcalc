## What is PT-141?

PT-141, also known as Bremelanotide, is a synthetic peptide derived from Melanotan II. Unlike Melanotan II, PT-141 has been specifically researched for its effects on sexual function in both male and female research models. It acts primarily on the melanocortin-4 receptor (MC4R) in the central nervous system.

## Research Dosing Parameters

- **Typical dose range:** 1–2 mg per injection

## How to Reconstitute PT-141

1. Use bacteriostatic water for reconstitution
2. Inject slowly along the inner vial wall
3. Gently swirl until fully dissolved
4. Store at 2–8°C after reconstitution
5. Use within 30 days

## FAQ

**What is the difference between PT-141 and Melanotan II?**
PT-141 is derived from MT-2 but has a cyclic structure that gives it different receptor selectivity. PT-141 focuses on MC4R (central sexual function), while MT-2 has broader melanocortin activity including pigmentation.
