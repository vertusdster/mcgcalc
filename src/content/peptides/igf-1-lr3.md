## What is IGF-1 LR3?

IGF-1 LR3 (Insulin-like Growth Factor-1 Long R3) is a synthetic, modified form of human IGF-1 with an arginine substitution at position 3 and an additional 13 amino acid extension at the N-terminus. These modifications reduce its binding to IGF-binding proteins (IGFBPs), resulting in a significantly longer half-life (~20–30 hours) compared to native IGF-1 (~12–15 minutes).

1. IGF-1 LR3 can be reconstituted with dilute acetic acid (0.1%) for better stability, or bacteriostatic water
2. Inject the liquid slowly along the vial wall
3. Gently swirl — never shake
4. Store at 2–8°C; use within 4 weeks
5. Protect from light and freeze-thaw cycles

## FAQ

**What is the difference between IGF-1 LR3 and native IGF-1?**
IGF-1 LR3 has ~3x lower affinity for IGFBPs and a much longer half-life (~20h vs ~15min), making it a more potent and longer-acting research tool than native IGF-1.
